Who is this guy?

Hello, My name is Adam Sandoval and I am a Graphic and Web designer based in Phonix Arizona. I was I graduated from Northern Arizona University in May of 2015 with a degree in Visual Communications with a dual emphasis in graphic design and interactive design. I like my type san-serif, I have a tendency to use lots of muted colors, I use Gotham too often, I prefer my paragraphs left-justified, and like my logos clean. My dream job is to be a tour guide at Jurassic Park but until that becomes a reality I just want to design. In all seriousness I want to help people in whatever job that I may end up in; be it through design work delivered or relationships built. I think design is a way to look at and solve problems and not just laying things out or crafting a logo. I want people to see my passion come through my design work. I don't just want to design - I want to educate, solve a problem, and have a call to action. I think that I can help people this way and hope to do so. I am open to any job in the design world but my preferred field is in logos and branding. I am sure that will change over the years but right now I am just excited to see what is out there and continue to learn.
I love to create anything from hand-drawn type, to handmade books, and pretty much everything in between. When I am not designing in front of a computer I am riding my bike, running, camping, or baking. I really enjoy being outside and experiencing nature I think it gives me balance to sitting inside and designing for hours on end. I also love America, my family, and Jesus.
